## The two skills

Lynx ships exactly two skills, both frozen at 14/14 detection accuracy.

### full-ui-experience-audit

Multi-screen sweep with auto-remediation loop. Use when auditing a whole app.

### ui-experience-audit

Single-screen drill-down. Use when targeting a specific failure.

The two compose: full delegates per-screen passes to per-screen via Phase 0h complementary-skill detection.

See [`skills/full-ui-experience-audit/SKILL.md`](https://github.com/krzemienski/lynx/blob/main/skills/full-ui-experience-audit/SKILL.md) and [`skills/ui-experience-audit/SKILL.md`](https://github.com/krzemienski/lynx/blob/main/skills/ui-experience-audit/SKILL.md) for the source-of-truth specs.
